Precinct 5.0

The Technological Solution for Predictive Crime Analysis with Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, and Machine Learning.

About the Project

Modernizing public safety in Pernambuco.

The Problem

Slow investigations, difficulty in identifying criminal patterns, and the lack of data integration hinder efficient crime prevention and resolution.

The Solution

A predictive analysis platform that supports the Civil Police, increasing case resolution rates, speeding up analysis, and identifying criminal hotspots.

Target Audience

Investigators, police intelligence sectors, universities, and research centers in public safety and data science.

Key Features

Predictive Heatmaps

Generates interactive geographical visualizations that show the intensity and concentration of crimes by neighborhood, allowing the identification of "hotspots" and assisting in preventive policing planning.

Crime Analysis and Classification

Allows classifying and grouping occurrences by type, modus operandi, and location. The system offers dynamic filters based on Machine Learning models (Random Forest, SVM) for in-depth analysis.

Dashboards and Reports

Provides interactive dashboards with charts and maps, in addition to allowing the export of reports in PDF and integration with Power BI for complementary and personalized data visualizations.
